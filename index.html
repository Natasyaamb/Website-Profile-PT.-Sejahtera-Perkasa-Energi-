PT SEJAHTERA PERKASA ENERGI

PT. Sejahtera Perkasa Energi is a company engaged in the trading of industrial fuel oil. Established since January 2, 2020, the company holds a license for wholesale trading of solid, liquid, gas fuels, and related products. We serve customers across South Sumatra Province, working to meet their set targets to ensure operational performance.
